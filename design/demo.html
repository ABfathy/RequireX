Requirements engineering, traced to source.

Two views into the same requirements doc.

RequireX extracts requirements from RFPs, transcripts, tickets and code, then keeps them traced to where they came from. Engineers work in the editor; clients review and sign in a calmer, simpler view.

The internal workspace. Codex-style three-pane layout: project & chat sidebar, central document with line numbers and inline evidence pins, right-rail for sources / chat history / version tree, chat composer at the bottom.
